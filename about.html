Methodology

Scrum

We use scrum because the requirements discussed at the start of the project could change anytime. Scrum gives us the flexibility of implementing those changes easier and much quicker than the traditional software development methods.

Release early. Release often

Scrum consists of multiple iterations or sprints. We make it our goal to ship at every end of a sprint so that your users get the shiny new features as quick and as often as possible.
